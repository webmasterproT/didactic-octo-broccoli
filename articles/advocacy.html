Advocacy

Advocacy is the act of speaking on behalf of or in support of another person, place, or issue. In our society, advocacy plays a crucial role in promoting change and bringing attention to important causes. Here at Chase White Rabbit, we explore the various facets of advocacy, its impact on society, and how individuals can become effective advocates for the issues they are passionate about.
